Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

General Questions

Q: Where are Cosmos Watches made?
A: Cosmos Watches are designed in Sweden, with materials sourced globally to ensure the highest quality.

Q: What makes Cosmos Watches unique?
A: Our watches are inspired by the beauty and mystery of the cosmos. Each piece is crafted with precision and designed to celebrate the wonder of the universe.

Q: Can I return a watch if I'm not satisfied?
A: Yes, we offer a 30-day return policy on all our watches, provided they are returned in their original condition.

Q: Do you offer international shipping?
A: Yes, we ship globally for free.

Q: How do I care for my watch?
A: Keep your watch clean with a soft, dry cloth. Avoid exposure to extreme temperatures and chemicals. For mechanical watches, regular servicing is recommended.
