History

The story of Cosmos Watches began long before the first design came to life. It started in 1986 with Bryar, a natural-born craftsman, innovator, and dreamer. Growing up, Bryar worked with his father delivering watch parts and tailoring custom suits for gentlemen. These formative years instilled in him an appreciation for timeless design, precision, and the beauty of fine craftsmanship.

Bryar’s journey didn’t stop there. Life took him through diverse paths—from working as a skilled car mechanic to pursuing dentistry, a field where his hands-on expertise and eye for detail truly shone. Upon completing his Doctor of Dental Surgery degree, he opened his own dental practice from scratch, building it into a thriving clinic that served patients for over seven years.

Yet Bryar’s curiosity and ambition couldn’t be confined to one field. In 2017, he made the bold decision to pivot and dedicate himself to a lifelong passion: watchmaking. Cosmos Watches was born out of his desire to craft something lasting, meaningful, and inspired by the elegance of the universe. Starting with a small studio in Stockholm, Bryar merged his knack for precision and love for design to create timepieces that reflect life’s most profound moments.

Why Cosmos?

As a child, Bryar would spend countless nights staring at the stars, captivated by the vastness of the cosmos and wondering what lies beyond. These moments sparked his curiosity about the universe and life’s purpose. This fascination with the infinite possibilities of the cosmos became the foundation for Cosmos Watches.

Each watch in the Cosmos collection is carefully named after celestial bodies, chosen for their uniqueness and beauty. Every detail, from the size to the color and materials, is meticulously crafted to echo the wonder of the universe. The brand isn’t just about time—it’s about the journey, the dreams, and the infinite possibilities that lie ahead.

Cosmos Watches represent more than timekeeping. They are a symbol of exploration, inspiration, and living boldly. Designed in Sweden, they are crafted using premium materials like 316L stainless steel, sapphire glass, and reliable Japanese movements, ensuring durability and elegance for every wearer.

For Bryar, naming the brand “Cosmos” wasn’t just a choice—it was an extension of his lifelong quest to think beyond boundaries, embrace curiosity, and celebrate the extraordinary.

Cosmos Watches is for the explorers, the dreamers, and those who dare to challenge convention. Whether you're celebrating milestones or chasing new ambitions, let Cosmos be your timeless companion, reminding you to always reach for the stars.

Crafted with precision. Designed to inspire. Welcome to Cosmos Watches.
