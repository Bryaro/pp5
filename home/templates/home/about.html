About Us

In the elegant district of Östermalm, Stockholm, the Cosmos Watches story unfolded in 2017, conceived by the creative mind of Dr. Briar, a dentist with a profound passion for simple and elegant timepieces. This journey wasn't merely a shift from dentistry to watch design; it was a harmonious extension of Dr. Briar's precision, patience, and appreciation for beauty into the realm of timekeeping.

Dr. Briar envisioned creating watches that went beyond being mere timekeepers to becoming symbols of life's understated luxuries and elegance. Originating in a modest studio, Cosmos Watches captured the essence of Stockholm—merging its historic allure with contemporary grace. Each design was intended not just as an accessory but as a lifelong companion, marking both significant and ordinary moments.

Quickly rising beyond its initial conception, Cosmos Watches became a sought-after brand, emblematic of Stockholm's luxurious simplicity and innovative spirit. Dr. Briar's creation evolved into a narrative that invites its bearers to value time and embrace a journey of passion. Cosmos Watches: where every tick is a tribute to the beauty of life's journey.
