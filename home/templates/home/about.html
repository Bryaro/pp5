History

In the elegant district of Östermalm, Stockholm, the Cosmos Watches story unfolded in 2017, conceived by the creative mind of Dr. Bryar, a dentist with a profound passion for simple and elegant timepieces. This journey wasn't merely a shift from dentistry to watch design; it was a harmonious extension of Dr. Bryar's precision, patience, and appreciation for beauty into the realm of timekeeping.

Dr. Bryar envisioned creating watches that went beyond being mere timekeepers to becoming symbols of life's understated luxuries and elegance. Originating in a modest studio, Cosmos Watches captured the essence of Stockholm—merging its historic allure with contemporary grace. Each design was intended not just as an accessory but as a lifelong companion, marking both significant and ordinary moments.

Quickly rising beyond its initial conception, Cosmos Watches became a sought-after brand, emblematic of Stockholm's luxurious simplicity and innovative spirit. Dr. Bryar's creation evolved into a narrative that invites its bearers to value time and embrace a journey of passion. Cosmos Watches: where every tick is a tribute to the beauty of life's journey.

The story of Cosmos Watches began with Bryar, a man whose journey reflects the boundless curiosity of the cosmos itself. Born in 1986, Bryar grew up immersed in craftsmanship, working alongside his father to deliver watch parts and tailor custom suits for gentlemen. These early experiences instilled in him a deep appreciation for design, quality, and precision.

One day, Bryar came across a verse that said, "God swears by time." This revelation shocked him—it was a profound realization that if God swears by time, then time must be the most valuable thing we have. This thought became a cornerstone of Cosmos Watches, inspiring Bryar to create timeless pieces that remind wearers to cherish every moment.

As demand grew and the brand gained popularity, Bryar expanded Cosmos Watches to reach a global audience. Recognizing the need for a broader platform, he built the official Cosmos Watches website himself, showcasing his skills not only as a designer but also as a full-stack developer. This transition allowed the brand to connect with customers worldwide, solidifying its reputation as a leader in elegant and timeless timepieces.

Why Cosmos?

As a child, Bryar often gazed at the stars, captivated by their beauty and mystery. He wondered about the universe's purpose and humanity’s place within it. This curiosity became the foundation of Cosmos Watches. The brand represents not just the passage of time but the infinite possibilities of what lies beyond.

Each watch in the Cosmos collection is named after celestial bodies, chosen for their uniqueness and elegance. The designs, materials, and names are carefully curated to reflect the majesty of the universe. Bryar’s fascination with the cosmos inspired him to create watches that are more than just timepieces—they are symbols of exploration, wonder, and sophistication.

Our Quality Promise

At Cosmos Watches, we pride ourselves on using the finest materials, such as 316L stainless steel, rust-free finishes, and scratch-resistant sapphire glass. Our timepieces are designed for durability, elegance, and precision. To ensure your satisfaction, we also offer quick and reliable shipping worldwide. Experience the perfect blend of luxury and functionality with Cosmos Watches.

Why a Simple and Classic Website?

At Cosmos Watches, we value simplicity and timeless design in every aspect of our brand, including our website. We believe that simplicity speaks volumes, allowing our products to take center stage without unnecessary distractions. Just as our watches embody classic elegance, our website reflects our commitment to a clean and sophisticated user experience.
