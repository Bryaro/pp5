About Us

Some stories begin with a single spark of curiosity. Ours started in 1986 with Bryar, a born innovator. Growing up, he worked alongside his father, delivering watch parts and tailoring custom suits for gentlemen who appreciated craftsmanship. It was here that Bryar learned the value of precision, elegance, and a strong work ethic.

But life never stood still for him. He went from tailoring suits to fixing cars, mastering the art of mechanics with the same hands-on passion. Yet, even as a car mechanic, Bryar’s ambitions kept growing. Dentistry became his next challenge—a career for those who value precision, detail, and craftsmanship, qualities Bryar embodied effortlessly.

In 2017, while practicing dentistry in the heart of Stockholm, Bryar’s journey came full circle. Inspired by his early days delivering watch parts, he founded Cosmos Watches. More than a brand, it was a vision: to create timepieces that celebrate not just time but the journeys that shape us.

Cosmos Watches are designed in Sweden, blending timeless craftsmanship with modern simplicity. Every piece, made with 316L stainless steel, sapphire glass, and precise Japanese movements, is built to last—just like the moments it marks. Each design reflects the elegance of the universe while honoring those who dare to dream big and live boldly.

Cosmos Watches isn’t about Bryar or his past—it’s about you. It’s about the dreamers, the explorers, and the changemakers who refuse to stand still. Whether you’re chasing your ambitions, exploring new horizons, or making memories with loved ones, Cosmos Watches is your companion on this incredible journey.

Wear the universe on your wrist. Embrace the time you have. This is Cosmos Watches—crafted for the bold, inspired by the stars.
