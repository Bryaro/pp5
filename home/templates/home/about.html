History

The story of Cosmos Watches began with Bryar, a man whose journey reflects the boundless curiosity of the cosmos itself. Born in 1986, Bryar grew up immersed in craftsmanship, working alongside his father to deliver watch parts and tailor custom suits for gentlemen. These early experiences instilled in him a deep appreciation for design, quality, and precision.

After graduating as a certified car mechanic, Bryar honed his skills in precision and problem-solving. His desire for mastery didn’t stop there—he pursued dentistry, a field where his attention to detail and steady hands found a new purpose. After earning his Doctor of Dental Surgery degree, Bryar opened his own practice from scratch, running it successfully for over seven years. But his entrepreneurial spirit and creative vision soon led him to explore new horizons.

In 2017, Bryar founded Cosmos Watches with a bold vision. He began by designing and crafting each watch himself, paying meticulous attention to every detail. At first, he personally delivered the watches to customers, building close relationships and earning their trust. The unique, elegant designs quickly gained traction, capturing the hearts of watch enthusiasts who valued both quality and individuality.

As demand grew and the brand gained popularity, Bryar expanded Cosmos Watches to reach a global audience. Recognizing the need for a broader platform, he built the official Cosmos Watches website himself, showcasing his skills not only as a designer but also as a full-stack developer. This transition allowed the brand to connect with customers worldwide, solidifying its reputation as a leader in elegant and timeless timepieces.

Why Cosmos?

As a child, Bryar often gazed at the stars, captivated by their beauty and mystery. He wondered about the universe's purpose and humanity’s place within it. This curiosity became the foundation of Cosmos Watches. The brand represents not just the passage of time but the infinite possibilities of what lies beyond.

Each watch in the Cosmos collection is named after celestial bodies, chosen for their uniqueness and elegance. The designs, materials, and names are carefully curated to reflect the majesty of the universe. Bryar’s fascination with the cosmos inspired him to create watches that are more than just timepieces—they are symbols of exploration, wonder, and sophistication.

The first collections drew inspiration from stars and planets, blending luxurious materials like 316L stainless steel and sapphire glass with bold yet elegant designs. Each piece tells a story, encouraging its wearer to embrace the beauty of the unknown and celebrate life’s journey.

From the stars above to the intricate craftsmanship of every watch, Cosmos Watches invites you to celebrate life’s journey. Whether you’re charting new paths or savoring quiet moments, let Cosmos be your timeless companion, reminding you that the universe is yours to explore.

Crafted with precision. Designed to inspire. Welcome to Cosmos Watches.
