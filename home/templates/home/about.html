History

The story of Cosmos Watches began with Bryar, a man whose journey is as diverse as the universe itself. Born in 1986, Bryar grew up in a family that valued craftsmanship and ingenuity. From a young age, he worked alongside his father, delivering watch parts and tailoring custom suits for gentlemen. These experiences planted the seeds of his appreciation for timeless design and meticulous attention to detail.

Graduating as a certified car mechanic, Bryar mastered the art of precision and problem-solving, skills that would later influence his approach to watchmaking. But he didn’t stop there. With an insatiable curiosity, he pivoted to dentistry, a field where his hands-on expertise and eye for perfection truly thrived. After earning his Doctor of Dental Surgery degree, Bryar built his own dental practice from scratch, running it successfully for over seven years.

Never one to settle, Bryar's journey took yet another turn. With a passion for technology and creativity, he became a full-stack developer, enabling him to build the very website that brought Cosmos Watches to life online. His ability to merge technical expertise with artistic vision laid the foundation for a brand that thrives on innovation.

In 2017, driven by his lifelong fascination with the cosmos, Bryar launched Cosmos Watches. Combining his varied experiences—mechanic, dentist, developer, and dreamer—he created a brand that celebrates precision, craftsmanship, and the boundless wonder of the universe. What started as a modest studio in Stockholm quickly became a beacon for those who value timeless elegance and bold exploration.

Why Cosmos?

As a child, Bryar often gazed at the stars, captivated by their beauty and mystery. He wondered about the universe's purpose and humanity’s place within it. This curiosity became the foundation of Cosmos Watches. The brand represents not just the passage of time but the infinite possibilities of what lies beyond.

Each watch in the Cosmos collection is named after celestial bodies, chosen for their uniqueness and elegance. The designs, materials, and names are carefully curated to reflect the majesty of the universe. The first collections were inspired by stars and planets, echoing Bryar’s lifelong fascination with the cosmos.

Cosmos Watches isn’t just about timekeeping; it’s about exploration, wonder, and embracing the extraordinary. Designed in Sweden, these timepieces are crafted using premium materials like 316L stainless steel, sapphire glass, and reliable Japanese movements, ensuring durability and elegance for every wearer.

From the stars above to the intricate craftsmanship of every watch, Cosmos Watches invites you to celebrate life’s journey. Whether you’re charting new paths or savoring quiet moments, let Cosmos be your timeless companion, reminding you that the universe is yours to explore.

Crafted with precision. Designed to inspire. Welcome to Cosmos Watches.
