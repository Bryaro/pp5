Quality & Certification

Precision. Craftsmanship. Excellence.

At Cosmos Watches, quality is at the core of everything we create. Each timepiece is meticulously crafted using premium materials, ensuring unmatched durability and precision.

Certified by TÜV SÜD, our watches pass rigorous quality control inspections before reaching your wrist.

Certified Quality Control

Every watch undergoes extensive inspection to meet the highest industry standards.

  • Workmanship & Aesthetics: Each watch is examined for perfection.
  • Movement Accuracy: Precision-tested for reliable timekeeping.
  • Material Durability: Crafted with 316L stainless steel and scratch-resistant sapphire glass.
  • Water Resistance Testing: Watches are pressure-tested up to 3 ATM.
  • Certification: Approved by TÜV SÜD for compliance and durability.

Certified by TÜV SÜD

The TÜV SÜD Pre-Shipment Inspection Report confirms that Cosmos Watches meet global quality standards.

  • Inspection Conducted By: TÜV SÜD Group
  • Inspection Result: Pass (Style, Workmanship, Functionality & Packaging)

Warranty & Assurance

We stand by our craftsmanship with a 2-year warranty covering manufacturing defects.

  • Battery Not Included: The warranty does not cover the battery.
  • Normal Wear & Tear: Expected aging is not included in the warranty.
