Quality & Certification

Precision. Craftsmanship. Excellence.

At Cosmos Watches, quality is not just a standard—it is a promise. Every timepiece undergoes meticulous inspection and testing to ensure precision, durability, and craftsmanship that stands the test of time.

We are proud to be certified by TÜV SÜD, a globally recognized authority in quality assurance. This certification confirms that our watches meet the highest industry standards.

Certified Quality Control

Every watch undergoes extensive pre-shipment inspection to ensure compliance with international benchmarks. Each piece is examined for aesthetics, movement accuracy, calibration, and waterproofing. Materials such as 316L stainless steel and scratch-resistant sapphire glass are rigorously tested to ensure durability. Every watch is pressure-tested up to 3 ATM to verify water resistance.

Certified by TÜV SÜD

The TÜV SÜD Pre-Shipment Inspection Report confirms that Cosmos Watches meet global quality standards. Every model undergoes a detailed assessment of style, workmanship, functionality, and packaging before approval.

Precision & Materials

Crafted with 316L stainless steel, Cosmos Watches are corrosion-resistant and built for longevity. Sapphire glass ensures maximum scratch resistance while maintaining exceptional clarity. Each timepiece is powered by a Japanese movement, ensuring precise and reliable timekeeping.

Tested & Proven Excellence

Our watches undergo AQL (Acceptable Quality Limit) inspections to maintain impeccable standards. Each model is tested for water resistance at 3 ATM, ensuring protection against daily exposure. Time accuracy is verified with a ±0.23 sec/day calibration margin. Designed for endurance, every watch is built to resist scratches and wear, even under demanding conditions.

Warranty & Assurance

Every Cosmos Watch is backed by a 2-year warranty, covering manufacturing defects. Please note that the warranty does not include battery replacements or standard wear over time. For further inquiries, visit our FAQ page or contact us.
