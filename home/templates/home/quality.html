Quality & Certification

At Cosmos Watches, quality is not just a standard—it is a promise. Every timepiece undergoes rigorous inspection and testing to ensure precision, durability, and craftsmanship that stand the test of time.

We are proud to be certified by TÜV SÜD, a globally recognized authority in quality assurance. This certification confirms that our watches meet the highest industry standards.

Certified Quality Control

Every watch goes through an extensive pre-shipment inspection to ensure compliance with international quality benchmarks.

  • Workmanship & Aesthetics: Each watch is examined for precision and perfection.
  • Functionality Testing: Movement accuracy, calibration, and waterproofing are all verified.
  • Material Inspection: Made with 316L stainless steel and scratch-resistant sapphire glass.
  • Water Resistance Testing: Watches are pressure-tested up to 3 ATM.
  • Certification: Approved by TÜV SÜD for compliance and durability.

Certified by TÜV SÜD

The TÜV SÜD Pre-Shipment Inspection Report confirms that Cosmos Watches pass all required quality assessments.

  • Inspection Conducted By: TÜV SÜD Group
  • Inspection Result: Pass (Style, Workmanship, Functionality & Packaging)

Precision & Materials

  • 316L Stainless Steel: Corrosion-resistant and durable.
  • Sapphire Glass: Scratch-resistant and ultra-clear visibility.
  • Japanese Movement: Engineered for accuracy and reliability.

Tested & Proven Excellence

  • AQL Standards: All watches are inspected following the Acceptable Quality Limit (AQL) standards.
  • Waterproof Testing: All models are tested for 3 ATM water resistance.
  • Time Accuracy Check: Verified with a ±0.23 sec/day calibration margin.
  • Scratch & Wear Resistance: Built for durability under extreme conditions.

Warranty & Assurance

Each Cosmos Watch is a testament to our commitment to quality. We offer a 2-year warranty covering manufacturing defects. However, please note:

  • The warranty does not cover the battery.
  • Normal wear and tear over time are not included under warranty.

For further inquiries, visit our FAQ page or contact us.
